Mitch

Mitch, my cat, is quite a character with his bright orange fur! He turned 6 years old this past September, so he’s had plenty of time to perfect his mischievous ways. Among his favorite activities are munching on Cheez-Its and barbecue chips—though I'm pretty sure that's not typical cat food! But that's not all; Mitch also loves to stir up trouble around the house, whether he's knocking things off counters, hiding in unexpected places, or ripping posters off the walls. Life is never dull with him around!
