Katniss

Katniss is a colorful calico cat who just turned 6 years old in July. She has a unique taste in snacks, loving nothing more than Popeyes biscuits and blueberry donut holes. Katniss has a playful side too; she enjoys wrestling with her buddy Mitch, which is always a fun spectacle! When she's not munching on treats or play-fighting, you can find her soaking up the sun or happily getting pats from anyone who's offering. She loves the warmth of the sunlight and the comfort of gentle strokes. Katniss is definitely a delightful and quirky companion!
