Kiki

Kiki, my brown tabby cat, is definitely one of a kind! She turned 13 years old on July 12th. Kiki is super spoiled and loves attention all the time. If you can't find her, she's probably lounging in her favorite spot - her cat tower. But there's one other place she can't resist: any kind of bag lying around! She just loves to snuggle into them. Kiki's clingy nature means she always wants to be near someone, especially if they're giving her some well-deserved petting or treats. She makes everyday fun with her quirky habits and loving personality.
